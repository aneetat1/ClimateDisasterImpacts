What is our project?

Welcome to our webapp! We are a group of eight rising seniors in high school who were selected to participate in the NASA STEM Enhancement in Earth Science internship during the summer of 2022. Our research focuses on visualizing climate disaster impacts on Texas and Australia from 2000 to 2022
