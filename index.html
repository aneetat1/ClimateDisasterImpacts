Our Mission

Our website strives to educate others about the negative effects of technology on the environment. We aspire to make more people passionate about the electronic side of climate change that most people don't talk about. Through our website, we want to create a community of climate change activists and provide organized resources in order to make physical change in the world. We want to bring more awareness to this serious topic and take action.
